Rally Support

Need help with Rally? Contact Fynko Technologies for account, attendance, privacy, or technical support.

Contact support

Email us with a short description of the issue, the email address on your Rally account, and your organization name if you are part of one.

fynkotechnologies@gmail.com

Account help

We can help with sign-in issues, invite codes, profile updates, and account deletion questions.

Attendance help

For check-in issues, include the program name, date, and whether you used GPS, QR code, or virtual attendance.

Technical support

For app problems, include your device type, iOS version, and what you were doing when the issue happened.

What to include

To help us respond faster, please include:

  • Your full name and Rally account email
  • Your organization name, if applicable
  • A clear description of the issue
  • Screenshots or exact error messages, if available
