Account Deletion

Delete Your Rally Account

You can permanently delete your Rally account at any time. This page explains how to do it in the app and what happens to your data.

How to delete your account in the app

Account deletion is available directly inside Rally — no need to contact support.

  1. Open the Rally app on your device
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to your Profile
  4. Open the Account section
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Re-enter your password to confirm your identity
  7. Confirm deletion — your account will be permanently removed

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, the following personal data is permanently removed or anonymized:

Permanently deleted or anonymized

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo
  • Push notification token
  • Login account (authentication credentials)
  • Personal profile information

What may be retained

In limited cases, some records may be kept after account deletion:

May be retained

  • Anonymized attendance records where required for organizational reporting
  • Security, audit, or legal records where necessary

Retention period

Any retained records are kept only for as long as necessary for legal, security, or legitimate organizational record-keeping purposes.

Request deletion by email

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you may submit a deletion request by emailing us directly. We will process your request promptly.

Send your request to:

Fynkotechnologies@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Rally account so we can locate and remove your data.
